# BMW iX - In Car Augmented Reality Working as an HMI developer and project manager for Basemark Automotive Services, I participated in the design, prototyping and development of the BMW iX Video AR application. Integrated with the BMW iX digital display, the Video AR mode enables a range of advanced driving assistance features such as guidance, navigation, lane keep and cruise control.

# PACCAR Trucks - Digital Instruments Cluster Participated in the prototyping and development of the Kenworth T680 and Peterbilt 579 Digital Instruments Clusters. The unified and modular Paccar Digital HMI platform implements a wide range of driver assistance and system maintenance utilities - tailored to fit the design and functional requirements of various OEMs.

# DAF XF/XG Trucks - Digital Instruments Cluster Participated in the prototyping and development of the DAF XF/XG Digital Instruments Cluster. The digital display was deployed in the next generation of DAF Trucks, in order to improve the safety, efficiency and maintenance of truck fleets.
